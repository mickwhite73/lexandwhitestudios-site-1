Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Lex & White Studios Pty Ltd respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle personal information for Web Protect Pro and related support services.

Who we are

Web Protect Pro is published by Lex & White Studios Pty Ltd.

Contact: support@lexandwhitestudios.com

Information we may collect

We may collect limited personal information when you purchase, activate, or request support for Web Protect Pro. This may include:

  • your email address
  • purchase or licence information
  • basic activation information needed to validate your licence
  • support messages you send to us
  • technical details you choose to provide, such as app version, Windows version, screenshots, or error messages

Payments

Payments are processed by Stripe or another payment provider. We do not receive or store your full payment card details.

Licence activation

Web Protect Pro may contact our licence server to validate a licence key and activation status.

We are designed not to store raw licence keys locally after activation. Activation data is used to help confirm valid licences and manage allowed device use.

Browsing privacy

Web Protect Pro is designed as a privacy-first browser. Lex & White Studios does not sell your browsing history.

We do not intentionally collect your private browsing content, passwords, or full browsing history through our website or licence system.

If you contact support, please do not send passwords, full payment card details, or private browsing information. Only send screenshots or URLs if they are needed to explain the issue and you are comfortable sharing them.

How we use information

We may use information to:

  • process purchases and licence activation
  • provide customer support
  • respond to refund or technical requests
  • improve Web Protect Pro and our website
  • help prevent misuse, fraud, or licence abuse
  • meet legal or business record requirements

Sharing information

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share limited information with service providers who help us operate Web Protect Pro, such as payment processors, email providers, hosting providers, licence systems, or support tools.

Data security

We take reasonable steps to protect information we hold. However, no website, software product, or online service can guarantee complete security.

Access and correction

You may contact us to request access to, or correction of, personal information we hold about you.

Contact us

For privacy questions, contact: support@lexandwhitestudios.com
