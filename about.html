About Us

Lex & White Studios Pty Ltd builds practical, privacy-focused software for everyday Windows users.

Who we are

Lex & White Studios Pty Ltd is the publisher of Web Protect Pro, a Windows desktop browser designed to give users clearer control over privacy, browsing safety, downloads, popups, and everyday web settings.

Our approach

We believe security tools should be clear, calm, and easy to understand.

Web Protect Pro is designed to improve browsing safety, reduce common web risks, and give users more control without turning the browser into something confusing or heavy.

What Web Protect Pro is designed to help with

  • reducing unwanted tracking and browsing noise
  • blocking or warning about risky downloads and unsafe actions
  • giving users privacy and DNS controls in one place
  • helping users manage popups, blocked sites, favourites, and quick URLs
  • keeping warnings calm and understandable

Important note

Web Protect Pro is a helpful safety layer, but no browser or software product can guarantee complete protection from every online risk.

Users should still take normal care when visiting websites, downloading files, entering passwords, making payments, and opening links.
